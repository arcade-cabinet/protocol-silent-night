# Holidaypunk Vision: Protocol: Silent Night

## 1. Identity & Tone
"Holidaypunk" is festive-industrial-menacing. High-contrast neon cyan/gold against cold indigo/soot. A viewport-filling wonderland that feels simultaneously joyful and oppressive.

## 2. Protagonists: The Present Factory
Players control anthropomorphic present boxes. 
- **Procedural Variety**: Every present is a unique combination of body shapes (Cylinder, Cube, Gift Bag), toppers (Trees, Star, Antlers), and flair.
- **Roster**: 50+ unlockable premade archetypes driven by a central PRNG-seeded factory.

## 3. Hostiles: Animated 3D Meshes
All enemies are fully articulated 3D character models. 
- **Animated Meshes**: Grinch-Bots, Reindeer-Rushers, and Gingerbread Golems are "meshy" and fully animated.
- **Former Operators**: Characters like Cyber-Elf and Mecha-Santa have transitioned to hostile forces, retaining their high-fidelity 3D meshes.

## 4. Roguelike Systems
- **Death is the Game**: Difficulty algorithms (Wave Formula) always win eventually.
- **Currency**: Cookies are the primary currency for upgrades.
- **Special Drops**: Scrolls provide high-impact, temporary or permanent boosts during a run.
- **Swarm Intelligence**: Enemy advancement and swarm rules are driven by deterministic algorithms based on player level and pressure.
